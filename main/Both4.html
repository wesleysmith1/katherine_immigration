"We will have more people enter our country illegally than were born in this country in one year."

Fact Check: According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, in 2017, more than 3.8 million births were recorded across the country. The Department of Homeland Security offers estimates for apprehension rates at the border and for how many people successfully enter the country illegally, based in part on observable data reported by agents on the ground and on statistical models. There were an estimated 104,000 ‘got aways’ in 2017 (an 83% decrease from 615,000 in 2006) and 160,000 "total successful unlawful entries" in 2017.
